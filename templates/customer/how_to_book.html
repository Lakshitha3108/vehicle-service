How to Book a Service

Step 1: Sign Up or Log In

To get started, please log in If you don't have an account yet, simply click on the "Sign Up" button and complete your registration. After signing up, you'll be automatically logged in.

Step 2: Access Your Dashboard

Once you're logged in, you will be taken to your dashboard where you can manage your vehicle services. On the dashboard, you'll find a button to Book a Service.

Step 3: Choose Your Vehicle

In your dashboard, you will be able to select the vehicle that needs servicing. Enter your vehicle's model and details, so we can match you with the appropriate services.

Step 4: Select a Service

Choose from a variety of services, such as oil changes, tire replacements, or general maintenance. Pick the service that best fits your needs.

Step 5: Pick a Date

Select your preferred date the service. Our system will show available slots, so you can pick a time that works for you.

Step 6: Confirm Your Booking

Review the details of your booking, including the service, vehicle, and timing. After confirming, your booking will be scheduled, and you will receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Step 7: Complete the Service and Make the Payment

Once the service provider has completed the service on your vehicle, the status will be updated to "Completed". You will receive a confirmation email with all the service details. After that, you will be able to click the "Pay Now" button to complete your payment for the service.
